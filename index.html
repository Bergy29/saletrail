About
About SaleTrail

SaleTrail turns garage sale day into something you can actually plan for, whether you're hunting for a bargain or hosting one yourself.

Plan your daySearch any address or suburb, filter by category, and heart the sales you want to hit. They all land in My Day, grouped so you can plan the shortest run between them.
Plan your tripsStart with the whole of New Zealand, zoom into your region, and build a Saturday route instead of driving in circles.
Make the most of a garage saleNew listings appear the second they're posted. Garage sales are first in, first served, so seeing it early is the whole game.
Protect your infoYour name, phone, and email are never public. Your address is only shown to paying members while your sale is live, then it's gone.
How it works
SaleTrail is one plan: $5 a month, free for your first 6 weeks with a payment method on file. It includes listing as many sales as you like from your own address, and browsing every sale on the map. Billed through your phone's app store or card, SaleTrail never sees your card details.
Terms and safety
Payments and cancellation
  • SaleTrail is a single $5 a month membership, free for your first 6 weeks with a payment method on file. It is billed in advance and is non refundable for the current billing period, including partial months.
  • Your membership includes listing as many sales as you like from your own address, and browsing every sale on the map. There is no separate fee for either.
  • You can cancel your membership at any time from your device's subscription settings or from Account in the app. Cancelling stops future billing; it does not refund the period you're currently in, and you keep access until that period ends.
  • All payments are processed by Apple, Google, or Samsung billing under their own refund policies, which may apply in exceptional circumstances at their discretion.
Your privacy
  • Everything you provide at signup, your name, address, phone number, email, and password, is strictly protected and used only to run your account and confirm the address of a listing.
  • Your personal details are never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers or other third parties.
  • Only your suburb and sale details are ever shown publicly. Your full name, phone number, and email stay private, and your exact address is only shown to signed in, paying members while a sale is live.
  • Card and payment details are handled entirely by Apple, Google, or Samsung billing. SaleTrail never sees or stores your card number.
  • You can request a copy of your data, or ask for your account to be deleted, at any time.
Disclaimers
  • SaleTrail lists sale times, locations, and categories that hosts submit themselves. We do not inspect, verify, or take responsibility for the items sold, their condition, price, or accuracy of any listing.
  • SaleTrail is not responsible for any loss, theft, or damage to belongings, before, during, or after a sale.
  • Meeting people at a private address carries inherent risk. SaleTrail does not vet hosts or visitors and is not responsible for the safety, conduct, or security of anyone using the app.
  • Attending or hosting a sale is entirely at your own risk. Use your own judgement: consider visiting in daylight, letting someone know where you're going, and trusting your instincts.
  • Hosts are responsible for the safety and legality of their own property, their sale, and any transactions that take place there.
